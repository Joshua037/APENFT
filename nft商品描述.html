Welcome to Phase two of my Fire, Water, Air, and Earth series in the ‘Elements’ Collections. This collection brings the element of water flowing into Opensea with a series of abstract Glacial River pieces shot throughout a 10-year timespan in Iceland. Each piece tells a unique story of our Earth’s glaciers as they journey for hundreds of years as ice, eventually melting and ending their centuries-long lifespan as these majestic dancing rivers flowing into the ocean.



Bringing environmental awareness through visual storytelling within the “Elements’ collection is this project's mission. That is why I have also partnered with Give Back to Nature where we will plant hundreds of trees with every sale. Adding to the fun, five collectors of "Glacial Rivers from Above" Pieces chosen at random will be given a print of their NFT!
